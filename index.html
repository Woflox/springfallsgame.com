Spring Falls is a puzzle game where you erode the landscape to direct the flow of water and grow flowers.

Soft visuals, ambient guitar work, and environmental sounds come together to create a meditative, relaxing experience.

Features

  • 60 hand-crafted levels
  • Unique & clever gameplay revolving around water flow
  • Lots of lovely flowers to nurture and grow
  • Ambient-folk soundtrack and soothing nature sounds
  • Gorgeous mountain vista backdrops
